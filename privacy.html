Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Bleachers & Seats application ("the App") collects, uses, and protects information. This App is an internal business tool for Bleachers & Seats LLC.

2. Information We Collect

The App collects and stores:

  • Work order and inspection data entered by users
  • Customer and location information for job tracking
  • Photos and documents uploaded for inspections
  • QuickBooks data (estimates, customers) when integration is authorized

3. How We Use Information

Information is used solely for:

  • Managing work orders, inspections, and scheduling
  • Generating estimates and tracking job status
  • Internal business operations and reporting

4. QuickBooks Data

When you connect QuickBooks Online:

  • We request read-only access to estimates and customer data
  • Data is used to import job history and synchronize records
  • We do not modify your QuickBooks data
  • You can disconnect QuickBooks access at any time through the App settings or your QuickBooks account

5. Data Storage

Data is stored securely using:

  • Vercel Postgres for structured data
  • Vercel Blob for images and documents
  • Upstash Redis for session tokens

All data is transmitted over HTTPS encryption.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties. Data is only accessible to authorized Bleachers & Seats personnel.

7. Data Retention

Business records are retained as needed for operations and legal compliance. You may request data deletion by contacting the system administrator.

8. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact the system administrator.
